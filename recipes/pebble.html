Pebble's Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Dry Cat Food
  • 1 scoop of wet food ( about half a can)
  • 3 tablespoons of water

Steps

  1. Add dry food to bowl
  2. Add wet food to bowl
  3. add water to bowl
  4. let sit for a 2 minutes while water absorbs into food He may scream this is a good time to mock him
  5. Feed the beast
