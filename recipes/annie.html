Annie's Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen stouffer's mac and cheese

Steps

  1. Open Box and throw it away
  2. Slice top plastic in three seperate vertical lines using a fork ( this matters you have to use a fork)
  3. Place in Microwave for 4 minutes
  4. Pull top plastic mostly off but not all the way
  5. Stir the middle of the mac and cheese only. Do not touch the edges
  6. recover mac and cheese
  7. put in microwave for addtional 4 minutes
  8. put on small plate
  9. find the smallest fork
  10. Feed the Beast
