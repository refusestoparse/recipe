Henry's Dinner

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of dry dog food
  • 1 scoop (about half a can) of wet cat food
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm water
  • frozen veg as feels right.

Steps

  1. Add Dry food to puzzle bowl
  2. add wet cat food to bowl
  3. add water to bowl
  4. let sit until he stops whining
  5. place in front of stove on floor
  6. make him sit and wait for however long seems right
  7. Feed the Beast
